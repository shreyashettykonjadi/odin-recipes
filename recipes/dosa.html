Udupi Masala Dosa

Description

Udupi Masala Dosa is a renowned South Indian dish originating from Udupi, Karnataka. This crispy, golden-brown dosa is filled with a spiced potato filling and is traditionally served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

For the Dosa Batter:

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1 cup idli rice
  • ½ cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 teaspoons toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 2 teaspoons chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • ¼ cup poha (flattened rice)
  • Salt, as needed

For the Red Chutney:

  • 3 tablespoons chana dal
  • 7 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 small onions (shallots)
  • Salt, as needed

For the Potato Filling:

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped carrots (optional)
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  • ⅛ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt, as needed

For Tempering:

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • ½ teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal
  • 2 teaspoons chana dal
  • 5 cashews
  • 1 sprig curry leaves

Steps

Preparing the Dosa Batter:

  1. Rinse the raw rice, idli rice, urad dal, toor dal, and chana dal together. Soak them in water for about 3 hours.
  2. Soak the poha separately for 15-20 minutes before grinding.
  3. Drain the soaked ingredients and grind them with the soaked poha and necessary water to achieve a smooth batter consistency.
  4. Add salt to the batter, mix well, and let it ferment overnight or for about 12 hours.

Preparing the Red Chutney:

  1. In a pan, dry roast the chana dal and dried red chilies until aromatic.
  2. Add the garlic cloves and shallots, sautéing for a couple of minutes.
  3. Allow the mixture to cool, then grind it with salt and a little water to form a thick, spreadable chutney.

Preparing the Potato Filling:

  1. Boil the potatoes until tender, peel, and roughly mash them.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add urad dal, chana dal, cashews, and curry leaves. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.
  3. Add the chopped ginger, green chilies, onions, and carrots. Sauté until the onions become translucent.
  4. Stir in the turmeric powder and salt.
  5. Add the mashed potatoes, mix well, and cook for a few minutes. Set aside.

Making the Masala Dosa:

  1. Heat a dosa tawa (griddle) and lightly grease it.
  2. Pour a ladleful of the fermented batter onto the center of the tawa and spread it outwards in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  3. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges and on top of the dosa.
  4. Once the dosa starts turning golden brown, spread a spoonful of the red chutney evenly over it.
  5. Place a portion of the potato filling in the center of the dosa.
  6. Fold the dosa over the filling and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
